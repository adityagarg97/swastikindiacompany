About us

Who We Are

Swastik India Company is an emerging trading company dealing in refrigeration gases and other air conditioning supplies.

We have experience in this field for past 20 years. Since our establishment in 2014, we have grown to be known as one of the leading refrigeration wholeseller as well as a leading distributer of air conditioning parts and equipments.

What We Do

We have been the wholeseller of refrigeration gases, compressor oils and spare parts. We also deal in air conditioning.

We are the authorized distributer of Floron gases in Daryaganj, Delhi. We are also the authorized reseller of Freon gases. In addition we also deal in

  • Butane Gas
  • Coil Cleaning Chemicals
  • Air Conditioning Oils
